About TED

TED (Technology, Entertainment, and Design) is a nonprofit organization that was founded in 1984. It started as a conference where people from these three industries could come together and share their ideas and experiences. TED has since grown to become a global platform for spreading ideas and knowledge, with events, talks, and initiatives taking place all over the world. The organization is committed to the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives, and ultimately, the world.

About TEDx

TEDx is a program that allows individuals and communities to organize independent TED-style events. These events are designed to spark deep discussions and connections at the local level. TEDx events are licensed by TED, but are independently organized by local organizers who apply to host the event. The "x" in TEDx stands for "independently organized TED event". TEDx events are held all around the world and cover a wide range of topics, from science and technology to business and culture.

About TEDxGITAMHyderabad

TEDxGITAMHyderabad is a TEDx event organized by the students of GITAM (Deemed to be University), Hyderabad. Our mission is to bring together bright minds to give talks that are idea-focused, and on a wide range of subjects, to foster learning, inspiration, and wonder. The event is a one-time opportunity to explore diverse ideas and perspectives from expert speakers. The goal is to inspire and motivate attendees to make a positive impact in their communities.
